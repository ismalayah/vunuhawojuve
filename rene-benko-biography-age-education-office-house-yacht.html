Ren Benko Biography; Age, Education, Office, House, Yacht

René Benko is an Austrian real estate, media and retail investor and founder of the Signa Holding.

René Benko Age

How old is René Benko? René Benko is 46 years old. He was born on May 20, 1977, in Innsbruck, Tyrol, Austria

René Benko Education

Where was René Benko educated? René Benko attended highschool. However, he did not graduate.

René Benko Office

Where does René Benko work? René Benko founded Signa Holding. The corporation is regarded as Austria’s largest privately held real estate group. Benko is a wealthy Austrian.

René Benko House

Where does René Benko live? René Benko lives mainly in Innsbruck, Tyrol

René Benko Yacht

Does René Benko own a Yacht? Benko owns the yacht “Roma” worth around 40 million euros
