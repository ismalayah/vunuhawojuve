Jared Fogle Biography

Jared Fogle was a former spokesperson for Subway, a popular fast-food restaurant chain. He gained notoriety for his remarkable weight loss success story, where he lost over 245 pounds by incorporating Subway sandwiches into his daily diet. As a result of his significant weight loss, he became the face of Subway’s advertising campaign and was affectionately known as the “Subway Guy.” Jared Fogle was born in Indianapolis, Indiana, on August 23, 1977, and attended Indiana University. He graduated with a degree in hospitality management in 2000.
