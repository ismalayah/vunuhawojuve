Alice Cooper Net Worth, Age, Girlfriend, Family, Biography &amp; More

Alice Cooper or Vincent Damon Furnier is an American who is a popular American singer, songwriter, and actor by profession. He is regarded as the “Godfather of Shock Rock” by music journalists and peers owing to his raspy voice and stage acts.

His acts incorporate several props and theatrical illusions such as pyrotechnics, guillotines, electric chairs, fake blood, snakes, baby dolls, and dueling swords. He drew inspiration from horror flicks, vaudeville, and garage rock to pioneer a macabre and theatrical form of music.

Alice Cooper Wiki/Biography

Born on 04 February 1948, Alice Cooper’s age is 74 years as of 2022. He was born and raised in a well-settled Christian family from Detroit, Michigan, United States. He is American by nationality and believes in Christianity religion.

He completed his early schooling at Cortez High School, United States.

After that, he enrolled himself at Glendale Community College from where he completed his graduation. From the very beginning of his childhood, he was more interested in music rather than in studying.
