The Shaved Ice Stand (Japanese: かき氷屋 Shaved Ice Stand) is a chain of stands located in Kitakami.

In the games

Shaved ice products purchased at the Shaved Ice Stand can be consumed to gain different types of Meal Powers.

Locations

Shaved Ice Stands are in the following locations:
